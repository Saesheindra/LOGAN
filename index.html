About LOGAN

Data that must be measured once and trusted for decades.

LOGAN — Land-Oriented Geomatics Analysis Network — is a professional geomatics and land surveying firm established to deliver precise, reliable and compliant surveying solutions for land, infrastructure and development projects.

The name represents a strong connection to land orientation, spatial accuracy and advanced surveying practice. The distinctive letter A in the wordmark — designed to represent a Total Station — symbolises precision measurement, technical expertise and the core foundation of the firm's operations.

We are small enough to give every client direct accountability, and equipped enough to handle work across the full geomatics spectrum — supporting government authorities, private developers, consultants and contractors.

P. Logeswaran is the founding Managing Director of LOGAN, leading the firm's engagements across cadastral, engineering, hydrographic, terrestrial and advanced geomatics surveys — with field experience built over years of survey work in Kuala Lumpur, Selangor and across Peninsular Malaysia.

He has executed subdivision and strata title surveys under the National Land Code, prepared Certified Plans for land and building partitions, and led underground utility detection campaigns on infrastructure corridors. He works closely with government agencies and licensed surveyors on projects requiring rigorous adherence to JUPEM standards and the Licensed Land Surveyors Act.

Services

The full geomatics spectrum.

A comprehensive range of land surveying and geomatics solutions, executed with precise instruments, advanced technology and proven methodologies.

01

Cadastral

Accurate boundary surveying for land subdivision, amalgamation, title applications and dispute resolution in compliance with land authority standards.

02

Engineering

Precise spatial data for construction setting out, infrastructure alignment, earthwork surveys, deformation monitoring and as-built documentation.

03

Airborne

Drone photogrammetry for large area mapping, orthophoto generation, terrain analysis, progress monitoring and volume calculations.

04

LiDAR

High-accuracy 3D laser scanning for terrain modelling, corridor mapping, asset inspection and complex environment surveys.

05

Geodetic

Control network establishment, GNSS surveys, datum transfer, benchmark verification and precise positioning systems.

06

Terrestrial

Ground-based topographical surveys, detail surveys, control establishment and utility mapping using total stations and GNSS.

07

Underground Utility

GPR and electromagnetic detection for utility mapping, subsurface investigation and infrastructure risk assessment.

08

BIM

3D modelling, clash detection, as-built documentation and construction coordination through integrated survey data.

09

GIS

Spatial analysis, asset management, geospatial database development and mapping solutions for data-driven planning.
